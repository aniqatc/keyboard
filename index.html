Did you know...

There's eight different keyboard themes that you can access on here?! Either press through the F1 to F8 buttons on your keyboard or click through the corresponding buttons on the screen with your mouse to change the theme!

If you're feeling particularly adventurous, then click the button at the top-right of the keyboard for a random theme!

F9 through F12 are reserved for either keyboard animations or additional themes. If you enjoyed this website, I'd love to know!
